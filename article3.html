The Philosphy of Positive Energy

“ One lives ones life as it happens. We all make choices that affect the course of our lives. We choose whether we give love, and feel loved. It is forever ours and our alone responsibility for happiness. The moment we put it onto anyone else, we have already lost the game. We decide whether we are happy or not, and how to be. Whether we decide to make this choice or not, is just something that we deal with. Just like having faith in the universe, it is just something we deal with. Part of this involves realizing to each their own. People will be who they are going to be, and it is not our responsibility to change them or tell them they are incorrect about what they are doing. Rather it is just acceptance for life, as all life is precious. As you make your choice, realize what you are getting into. It takes hard work, it takes commitment. It takes dedication. It will not come easy. Once you have it to, you must stay eternally vigilant. Anyone who tries to come between you and your happiness is not worth keeping around. ……………………………
